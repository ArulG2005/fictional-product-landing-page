Sekyo S1 Smart Watch Phone For Kids

Introducing the Kid's First Smart Phone - the ultimate safety smart watch designed exclusively for kids, providing parents with peace of mind and children with endless fun and learning opportunities.

Two Way Calling And Voice Chat

S1 Smart Watch has a 2-way calling feature, it means that not only can the child receive calls from predefined contacts (such as parents), but they can also make calls to those contacts using the smartwatch. This functionality can be a valuable communication tool for parents and guardians to stay in touch with their children, especially in situations where giving them a smartphone might not be suitable.

Flashlight

A flashlight feature on a smartwatch can be handy in low-light situations or emergencies.
The presence of a front camera allows the user (in this case, a child) to take photos. It can add an element of fun for kids who enjoy capturing moments or interacting visually with others.
